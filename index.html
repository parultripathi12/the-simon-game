The Simon Game

Rules of the Game:

Level 1. The computer will flash a random button. Click on that button.

Level 2. The computer will flash another random button.Click on the button that you previously clicked in Level 1 and then the button that computer flashed just now.

So, the computer will flash one button in each level. You have to remember all the previous clicks done from Level 1 to the current Level and click on the butttons in the same order.
